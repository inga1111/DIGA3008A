Design section

Goal aesthetic

My goal aesthetic as a single statement is bold vintage glamour or 2000s bold glamour with touches of leopard print. In vintage fashion especially, animal print was seen as a status symbol and considered luxurious, I am against the use of real animal skin for ethical reasons but I am intriuged by the visual appeal of animal skins, and I believe we can make beautiful things using the patterns found on these animals so long as it is faux.

I want to go for something reminiscent of the 70s-90s and early 2000s glamour diva / beauty queen style with a lot of animal print accents and very feminine textures. All of this but with a touch of modern style and minimalism. Also with a touch of jungle feel with the exotic flowers and wild animals.

Color Swatches

my colour palette consists mostly of pinks , creams, black and animal print.

Font Samples
